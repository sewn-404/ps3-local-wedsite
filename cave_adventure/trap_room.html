The Spike Pit

You squeeze through the crack and slide down a dusty chute, landing hard in a cramped stone corridor. At the end of the corridor, the floor drops away into a massive **spike pit**.

A small sign is etched above two pressure plates on the wall:

The sign reads: "The second choice is always best."

1. Press the LEFT Plate.

2. Press the RIGHT Plate.

3. Try to climb back up to the Main Hall.

The Silent Guardian

You pry at the seam and a hidden door swings inward. Inside stands a stone statue of a stern guardian holding an obsidian key. Its eyes glow faintly.

An inscription reads: "Answer with care, or leave what you cannot bear."

Riddle: I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have nobody, but I come alive with wind. What am I?

1. A Shadow

2. An Echo

3. Step back and leave the statue to its silence.

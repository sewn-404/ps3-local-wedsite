The Chamber of the Sun

The air here is warm. The walls are covered in shimmering crystals that catch the light, but the path ahead is blocked by a small, stone pedestal.

Etched onto the pedestal is a riddle that must be solved to proceed:

What has cities, but no houses; forests, but no trees; and water, but no fish?

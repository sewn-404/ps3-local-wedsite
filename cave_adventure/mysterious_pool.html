The Shimmering Pool

You follow the faint footprints to a small underground pool. The water glows faintly and reflects images not quite your own.

On the far side, a narrow path continues deeper, half-hidden behind a curtain of draping roots.

Choose an action:

1. Cup water and drink (mystery).

2. Cross the path behind the roots (leads to hidden places).

3. Return to the cave entrance.

You drink:

The water tastes like cool rain and gives you a fleeting vision of a sunlit room filled with maps. You feel calmer, as if your choices will be clearer.

Continue toward the place in your vision.

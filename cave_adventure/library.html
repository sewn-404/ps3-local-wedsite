The Hidden Library

Behind a sliding shelf you discover a small library, its shelves filled with dust-covered tomes and scrolls. A single book lies open on a lectern with a note: "Knowledge opens doors; curiosity opens paths."

A dusty ladder leads down and a rolled map rests beside the lectern.

What do you examine?

1. Read the open book (A helpful hint appears).

2. Take the rolled map and follow its path directly to the Treasure.

3. Return quietly to the Main Hall.

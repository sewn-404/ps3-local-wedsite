The Main Hall

The smooth tunnel leads to a vast, echoing hall. Ahead are two carved wooden doors.

The door on the left is adorned with symbols of the sun; the door on the right with symbols of the moon.

Between the doors, engraved into the floor, are faded runes that hint at a place of knowledge nearby.

*You also notice a dark, narrow crack in the wall, easily missed, that seems to lead down.*

Which door will you enter?

1. The Sun Door (Left).

2. The Moon Door (Right).

3. Squeeze through the dark crack in the wall (New Path).

4. Inspect the runes (Follow the hint to a hidden library).

4. Go back to the entrance.
